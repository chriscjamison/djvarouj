About DJ Varouj

DJ Varouj aims to please.

His music reflects the audience, setting, and ambiance. He is a master of harnessing his talents to evoke emotions from the crowd that only he can provide. From his experiences performing in front different audiences ranging from church formal proms to birthday parties and weddings, his music ignites an emotion that others cannot compete with.

His use of mixing different genres of music from Hip Hop to Armenian and today's hits blends different styles while retaining the harmonies and melodies and beat that make the music emotionally appealing. His beats are clean. Not overbearing, but filled with melody and unique transitions that allow songs to move seamlessly, one into another.

His caring and approaching personality is just as amazing as his talent.
